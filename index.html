FAQ

Questions

Do I need a biology degree to play? +
Not at all. Puzzles range from accessible to expert level. If you're a curious A-level student or a working researcher, there's something for you. The explanations after each puzzle teach you the science regardless of your starting point.
Is it free? +
Completely free. One daily puzzle for everyone, plus unlimited practice mode across all four categories. No account required.
How often is new content added? +
New puzzles are added regularly. The daily puzzle rotates through a growing library — suggest a topic you'd like to see via the feedback link below.
Can I use Biodle to study for exams? +
Yes — that's one of the main reasons it was built. The clue-by-clue format mirrors the active recall technique that's proven to boost retention. Many students use it as a daily warm-up before revision sessions.
I'm a lecturer — can I use this with my students? +
Absolutely. Share the daily puzzle link with your class at the start of a lecture or as a warm-up exercise. Get in touch if you'd like a category built around your module's content.
